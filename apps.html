AMBUS - embassies of the United States of America - is an useful and necessary app during travelling Europe. AMBUS contains several pieces of information about American embassies and consulates (phone numbers, addresses, e-mails, www, basic maps.

All informations is implemented inside the app so you do not need to care about (mobile phone) signal in faraway places. Everything is in your phone.

safe road

  • category: Travel
  • updated: 10 September 2012
  • version: 1.0
  • size: 9.2MB
  • language: English
  • developer: Paweł Pabisiak
  • rated: 4+

safe road - it is an app with collection of the most important information related with travelling in Europe by car. The app consists a database with following pieces of information:

- speed limit in built-up area, outside of the area on express and highways;

- necessary equipment that should be in a car;

- information about paid roads;

- additional information about admissible amount of alcohol in blod, main prohibitions and rules;

- calculator of speed (converter of miles/h to kilometers/h and back).

The app doesn not need internet connection

Updated 10-th September 2012

Sources of information:

- http://honda.pl

- www.regiomoto.pl

- www.pzmtravel.com.pl

requirements: Compatible with iPhone 3GS, iPhone 4, iPhone 4s, iPod touch (3rd generation), iPod touch(4th generation) and iPad Requires iOS 5.0 or later.

Unia Europejska Informator

Unia Europejska Informator

  • category: Education
  • updated: 25 January 2016
  • version: 2.0
  • size: 8.9MB
  • language: English, German, Polish
  • developer: Paweł Pabisiak
  • rated: 4+

European Union Info is an compedium of basic information connected with European Union (EU). Now you can have answers for unexpected question about the union countries close at hand.

The compedium contains data about: capital city, total area, population, currency, important dates and others. Besides that you can find information how UE "works": the structure, the main roles and you have a posibility to hear the European Union anthem.

Unia Info jest aplikacją, informatorem zawierającym podstawowe informacje na temat Unii Europejskiej. Dzięki tej aplikacji możesz mieć odpowiedzi na niespodziewane pytania dotyczące Unii zaraz pod ręką. Kompedium zawiera dane państw Unii, takie jak: nazwę stolicy, liczbę mieszkańców, walutę, ważne daty dotyczące integracji z Uni i inne. Oprócze tego dowiesz się o historii powstania Unii, jej struktury i zadaniach a nawet posłuchasz hymnu Unii
